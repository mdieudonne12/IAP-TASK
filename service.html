Frontend Development

Frontend development is all about building the parts of a website that people see and interact with. It includes everything from layout and design to making buttons, menus, and animations work smoothly. As a frontend developer, I use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create clean, responsive, and user-friendly websites. My goal is to turn ideas and designs into real, working web pages that look good on any device and provide a great experience for users.
